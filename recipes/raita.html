Raita Recipe to Serve with Chicken Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1 small cucumber (grated or finely chopped)
  • 1 small onion (finely chopped)
  • 1 small tomato (finely chopped, optional)
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped, optional)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh coriander leaves (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon fresh mint leaves (chopped, optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon black salt (or regular salt, to taste)
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • A pinch of red chili powder (optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala (optional)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the Yogurt: In a medium bowl, whisk the yogurt until smooth and creamy.
  2. Add Vegetables: Add the grated or chopped cucumber, onion, and tomato to the yogurt. Stir well.
  3. Season: Add roasted cumin powder, black salt, black pepper, and a pinch of red chili powder (if using) for a slight kick. You can also add chaat masala for extra flavor.
  4. Add Herbs: Mix in chopped fresh coriander and mint leaves for freshness.
  5. Adjust Consistency: If the raita is too thick, add a little water to reach your desired consistency.
  6. Chill and Serve: Let the raita chill in the fridge for at least 10-15 minutes before serving. Serve it cold alongside Chicken Biryani.
